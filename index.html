National Parks Visitation

About

The National Park System preserves natural and cultural resources on over 85 million acres of public land. Over 330 million visits were made to National Park Service units in 2017. On this map, markers at the park center point represent average annual recreational visits, measured from 1998 to 2017. Data was provided by National Park Service Open Data and Integrated Resource Management Applications Portal. Statistics are not available for every unit. Map authored by Robin Bruns
